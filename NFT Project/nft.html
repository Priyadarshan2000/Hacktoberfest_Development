About Us

Bald n Beautiful is a collection of 10,000 unique NFT art works stored on the Ethereum Blockchain. The collection honours the beautiful ladies from all over the world who have been fighting Cancer. BnB will provide the contributor with a one-of-a-kind representation of art while also contributing to cancer prevention, awareness, and diagnosis. Join us on our mission as we share the information in both the physical and digital worlds and help cancer warriors all across the world.

Wanna Ask Something?

When and how many tokens will be minted?

The supply is limited to 10,000 tokens, and the mint date will be announced shortly.

Is there a pre-sale for the Whitelist?

Yes! Our early supporters and active members of our Discord Community will be included to our Whitelist and will be allowed to mint the BnB NFTs before the public sale There are still a number of options to be on the Whitelist, such as giveaways , raffles, etc. Please visit our Discord channel for more information.

Please clarify on the BnB Project!

BnB is a creative and unique NFT project that exists not only to generate profit margins but also to give back to those who are "real life Warriors." We are here to help cancer societies and research institutes in all the way we can. We hope you will join us and help us make a difference in the NFT-space. More information is available under roadmap.

How can I get in touch with the team?

You can contact us via Discord or social media.

Where can I find more info?

Our website will be updated on a regular basis, however if you have any more queries, please send us a DM on Twitter or contact our Team via Discord.

Is there going to be any tokens withheld from the sale?

A total of 400 tokens will be allocated for the Team (120 tokens), as well as for marketing and prizes, including presents for our Angel Donor, whom we will encourage as part of our Roadmap
